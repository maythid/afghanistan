The Taliban

The Taliban came to prominence in Afghanistan in the autumn of 1994 and refers to itself as the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan. They were basically very extreme Muslims. 

The Taliban's promise/ purpose was to restore peace and security and enforce their own austere version of Sharia, or Islamic law, once in power.
They introduced/ supported Islamic punishment which included things like: public executions of convicted murderers

Men were required to grow beards and women had to wear the all-covering burka.

The Taliban was mainly supported by ethnic Pashtuns

Several Taliban leaders have been killed from US drone strikes throughout the years.
